About the Cheffette

This is the food
of my family and
my heritage.

I'm a self-taught cook who grew up watching my mother and yiayia fill the kitchen with the aromas of oregano, olive oil, and slow-roasted lamb. The Real Greek Cheffette is my way of keeping those flavours alive — and sharing them with you.

Every recipe here is made with the same love, the same pinchy-pinch of seasoning, and the same belief that good food brings people together.
